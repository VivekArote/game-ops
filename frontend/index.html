Ingested Risk Evaluations

  • Rule 1

    Abnormal Score Rate

    Score per minute exceeds 5,000.

    score / (match_duration_seconds / 60) > 5000
  • Rule 2

    Abnormal Kill Velocity

    Kills per minute exceeds 100.

    kills / (match_duration_seconds / 60) > 100
  • Rule 3

    God-Mode & Aimbot Metric

    Player has 100 or more kills with zero deaths in a single match.

    kills >= 100 && deaths === 0
  • Rule 4

    Instant High Score Hack

    Match duration is under 2 minutes (120 seconds) and score is over 50,000.

    match_duration_seconds < 120 && score > 50000

Action & Mitigation Tiers

Tier 1: Normal (Risk 0 - 1)

Clean match history. Fully eligible for regional leaderboard rankings and standard matchmaking pools.

Tier 2: Review (Risk 2)

Subtle telemetry anomalies. Kept on leaderboards, but highlighted in Security Audits for manual game master review.

Tier 3: Suspicious (Risk 3+)

Severe cheats detected. Instantly and automatically blacklisted from all public leaderboards and isolated from matchmaking queues.
